More about the farm visit experience

Our farm visit is shaped around real, humane, open-air farming. Guests often discover how free-range eggs, fresh produce, and farm standards come together in a calm, transparent setting.

We focus on welfare-first practices, clean handling, and the everyday life of the farm.

Frequently asked questions

Everything visitors usually ask before coming

Who is this farm visit suitable for?

The visit is ideal for families, food enthusiasts, and anyone curious about ethical farming, free-range eggs, and how farms operate in practice.

What should I wear or bring?

Comfortable footwear and light clothing are best. We recommend carrying water, sun protection, and a camera if you’d like to capture your visit.

How many people can book at once?

Our slots are managed in small groups to keep the visit personal and comfortable. Capacity is displayed during the booking step for each slot.

Is the booking verified?

Yes. We verify the visitor contact using OTP-based validation and collect attendee details for planning and safety records.
